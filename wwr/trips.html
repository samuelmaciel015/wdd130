Trips Descriptions

Cascade Conquerors

Feel the thrill of surging rapids and crisp mountain air as you paddle through the heart of the canyon! The Cascade Conquerors is perfect for first-time rafters and thrill-seekers alike. With a mix of fast-paced whitewater and scenic calm stretches, you’ll enjoy the perfect balance of excitement and relaxation.

Double Trouble

Unwind with a relaxing late-afternoon float as the sun dips behind the mountains. Double Trouble offers a peaceful rafting experience with gentle currents and golden-hour views that are perfect for photos. This trip is ideal for couples, families with young children, or anyone looking for a laid-back introduction to rafting.

Gullivers Travels

Get ready to conquer some of the most challenging rapids in the region! Gullivers Travels is our most adrenaline-fueled trip, designed for experienced rafters and true adventure junkies. Expect powerful waves, tight turns, and steep drops as you navigate Class IV and V rapids.
